# Music Streaming Hub Documentation

Welcome to the Music Hub documentation. This site tracks the current desktop beta, the iOS TestFlight flow, and the source build workflow for the rest of the stack.

## What is Music Streaming Hub?

Music Hub is a self-hosted music streaming system that turns your computer into a personal streaming server for your own library. No subscriptions, no uploads, complete control.

### Key Features

- **100% Private**: Your music stays on your devices
- **Zero Cost**: Free and open source forever
- **Stream Anywhere**: desktop hub plus iPhone companion beta
- **All Formats**: MP3, FLAC, AAC, ALAC, WAV, OGG, and more
- **DJ Features**: Rekordbox integration, BPM detection, smart playlists
- **Open Source**: Fully transparent, community-driven
